How does Optiboot work?

07.07.2020

Optiboot is a nice alternative for default Arduino bootloader. It has a few benefits to use - a little bit of space saved, sketches could be bigger, faster firmware uploading, many supported platforms. I thought it would be interesting to tear down its code and how it works as optiboot is quite small but is excellent example of principles of bootloader.

I'm not a developer of optiboot so I can miss something. Take this article and everything on the internet with grain of salt.
